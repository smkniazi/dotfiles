Move Focus
==========

Move the window foucs up    : Super+K
Move the window foucs right : Super+L
Move the window foucs down  : Super+J
Move the window foucs left  : Super+H


Keyboard Shortcuts
==================

Move to top right corner    : Ctrl+Super+2
Move to top left corner     : Ctrl+Super+1
Move to bottom right corner : Ctrl+Super+4
Move to bottom right corner : Ctrl+Super+3

Move to top                 : Ctrl+Super+K
Move to right               : Ctrl+Super+L
Move to bottom              : Ctrl+Super+J
Move to left                : Ctrl+Super+H

Move to the right screen    : Shift+Ctrl+Super+L
Move to the left screen     : Shift+Ctrl+Super+H
